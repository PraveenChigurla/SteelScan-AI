Insight: Severe Class Imbalance

"Other_Faults" dominates the dataset (34%), while "Dirtiness" and "Stains" are severe minorities (<4%). The LightGBM model utilizes `is_unbalance=True` to heavily penalize misclassification of rare faults, ensuring robust Macro F1 scores over plain accuracy.

    Insight

    Most geometric features exhibit a strong right skew. Tree-based models like LightGBM are invariant to monotonic transformations, so scaling or log-transforms are not strictly necessary.
